## Description
> Markha Valley Trek, a 3-night and 4-day is a moderate-level trek. Highlights of the trek are monasteries, monks, and breathtaking landscapes. The Markha Valley Trek is a great experience, where you'll meet locals, learn about their culture, and soak in the scenic beauty. Enjoy interactions with village communities and embrace the stunning natural views. The trek reaches its highest point at Kongmaru La, standing tall at 5,100 meters, offering breathtaking panoramic views.
