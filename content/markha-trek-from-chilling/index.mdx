## Description
> Markha trek, definitely one of the most popular trek of Ladakh.  

## At a glance

| DATE   | SERVICE                                             | ACCOMMODATION                  |
| -------|-----------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| DAY 1  | Arrive Leh, Welcome to Ladakh! Acclimatize and rest. | Hotel in Leh town - Meals: LD  |
| Day 2  | Pickup at hotel - Tsemo Gompa - Leh Palace - old town (LAMO, traditional bakery, etc.) - Main Bazaar (Lunch at Tibetan Kitchen) - visit Shankar Gompa - visit Shanti Stupa – transfer to hotel. | Hotel in Leh town - Meals: BD|
| Day 3  | Pickup at guesthouse, drive Leh - Magnetic Hill - Sangam - Skyu, Trek Skyu – Markha | Camping - Meals: FI            |
| Day 4  | Trek Markha  – Tachungtsek        | Camping - Meals: FI              |
| Day 5  | Trek Tachungtse – Nimaling | Camping - Meals: FI |
| Day 6  | Trek Nimaling – Gongmaru la – Shang Sumdo, pickup, drive Shang Sumdo - visit Hemis Gompa - Sakti, arrive at hotel |Hotel  in Sakti - Meals: FI |
| Day 7  | Drive Sakti – Pangong, arrive at Costy tents |Costy tents - Meals: FI |
| Day 8  | Drive Pangong – visit Chemde Gompa - visit Thiksey Gompa - Leh, arrive at hotel | Meals: FI |
| Day 9 | Departure - On time transfer you to Leh airport for your onwards journey, taking back good memories. | |

GLOSSARY
- FI = Fully inclusive
