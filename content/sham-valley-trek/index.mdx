## Description
> Likir to Temisgam trek, also know as baby trek. An easy route suitable for all trek enthusiasts. This trek offers the choice of homestays or camping. Commencing from Likir and ending at Lamayuru, on this trek experiance local lifestyle, cultural heritage, and stunning  landscapes of Ladakh.  

## At a glance

| DATE   | SERVICE                                             | ACCOMMODATION                  |
| -------|-----------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Day 1  | Pickup at guesthouse, drive Leh - Magnetic Hill - Sangam - Skyu, Trek Skyu – Markha | Camping - Meals: FI |
| Day 2  | Trek Markha - Tachungtse        | Camping - Meals: FI |
| Day 3  | Trek Tachungtse – Nimaling | Camping - Meals: FI |
| Day 4  | Trek Nimaling – Kongmaru la – Shang Sumdo, pickup, drive Shang Sumdo - visit Hemis Gompa - Leh, arrive at hotel | |


GLOSSARY
- FI = Fully inclusive
