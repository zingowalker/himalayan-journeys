## Description

> Explore Rajasthan's captivating treasures on this 7-day voyage, experience the local culture, the blue city of Jodhpur, the Amber Fort in Jaipur, the Golden City of Jaisalmer, and beyond. Safari the Thar desert atop camels, pass through the ancient alleys of Chandni Chowk in Delhi via cycle rickshaw, and the famous Jama Masjid, one of India's largest mosques. This journey weaves a unique experiences hopefully to last for long..
