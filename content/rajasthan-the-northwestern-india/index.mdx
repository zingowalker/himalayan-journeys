## Description

> This 7-days Explore Rajasthan's captivating treasures on a voyage encompassing the cerulean abodes of Jodhpur, the resplendent Amber Fort in Jaipur, the enchanting "Golden City" of Jaisalmer, and beyond. Traverse the desert atop camels, meander through the ancient alleys of Chandni Chowk in Delhi via rickshaw, and behold the grandeur of Adhai Din Ka Jhonpra, among India's largest mosques. This immersive journey is a tapestry woven with vibrant experiences across this majestic region..
