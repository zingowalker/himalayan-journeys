## Description

> This 7-day Ladakh's journey focuses on the unique Himalayan scenery and beautiful monasteries. Explore the architectural splendor of Thiksey Gompa, one of the largest in the region. Ride double hump camels in Nubra valley, witness the breathtaking azure panorama of Pangong Tso, and lots more on this seven days trip designed for travelers who want to make the most of their trip to this remote region of India.
